Who are we?

Adarsh Jan Seva Mandal is a non-profit grassroots social services organisation based out of Mumbai. Since our inception in 1977, we have consistently strived to empower the needy in India.

Our Story

One day Mr. Haridwar Singh had gone to meet his teacher friend in a municipal school, there he saw the teacher was scolding and beating his student for being absent for many days. After much persuasion, the child revealed that he couldn't afford the school uniform because of which he was absent. Haridwar Singh who witnessed this was moved by the incident and decided to his level best that no needy student is deprived of education due to monetary troubles like the uniform, books, etc. Hence along with like-minded people namely Late Mr. Indradeo Singh (President Awardee), Yadunath Rai, and others founded Adarsh Jan Seva Mandal. The organization was duly registered under the Societies Registration Act on 10 August 1977.

What We Do?

  • Child Education Resources

    Many Indian students drop-out of school because of lack of money. We fund their education and help them restart their academic journey. We provide school uniforms, bags and books to these students every year in the month of June. In addition, we also offer monetary assistance to the kids who excel academically.

  • Food Donation Drive

    We organise food donation drives at various places like old age homes, orphanages and hospitals. The aim is to help families struggling with food scarcity by providing nutritious food in adequate quantities preventing spoilage.

  • Felicitate Social Workers

    We felicitate social workers who have selflessly worked hard for the betterment of the society. We support and encourage such social workers who help set an example for future generations.

  • Environment

    We undertake tree plantation programs and also carry out cleanliness drives in different parts of the city. We also organise programs to enlighten the people about their responsibility towards the environment.
